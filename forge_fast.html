Forge.Fast’s Corporate Venture Studio-as-a-Service model leverages proven strategies and operating models namely Open Innovation, Startup Innovation, Venture Building and Venture Capital, and their convergence with Corporate Strategy for unlocking business value and gaining competitive advantage across the lifecycle of seeding, accelerating, and scaling industrial tech ventures.

Forge’s CVS Compass is a unified strategic planning framework for maximising value through Corporate-Startup engagements - offering an all encompassing visualisation tool to plan, execute, manage and govern strategic corporate innovation through corporate venturing programs. The Compass leverages a STRATEGY SANDBOX at its core, which serves as the contextual lens to implement VALUE CREATION PROGRAMS (Startup Engagement Programs). These programs are selected, scoped and sequenced for implementation are tied to VALUE CAPTURE MODELS (Startup Partnership Models) for unlocking financial, commercial, organisational, and operational value. Ultimately the governance and management of the portfolio of startup engagement programs and startup partnership models for maximising enterprise value are structured as VALUE GENERATION PATHWAYS

STRATEGY SANDBOX

Serves as the foundational phase for a Corporate Venture Studio, involving the development of a comprehensive strategy framework aimed at maximising transformational outcomes, in alignment with and complementing the company’s overarching vision for business growth & transformation.

By developing the Industrial Major’s (Corporate) transformation strategy and articulating the rationale and strategic themes thereof, the Strategy Sandbox serves as the point of origin and defines the guiding principles for corresponding value creation programs and value capture models.

TECH-POWERED INDUSTRIAL DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION FRAMEWORK

The Sandbox implements a Portfolio Management Strategy that outlines a structured framework for effectively managing a portfolio of concurrent value creation programs and value capture models, encompassing various elements including portfolio diversification, risk management strategies, evaluation criteria, etc to maximise transformation outcomes and RoI, as well as determining the Strategic Prioritising [Scope, Span & Sequence], Horizon Planning, Capital Allocation towards multiple Value Generation Pathways that create, capture and generate enterprise value from startup engagements & technology development.

This is in turn supported by an Integration Readiness Checklist, offering a structured approach to evaluate integration opportunities for potential startup partners into the operations and ecosystem, thereby mitigating risks associated with integration challenges.

Airbus' Innovation Scouting & Industrialisation program, a platform under Airbus Scale's Open Innovation vertical, aims to accelerate joint-industrialisation projects by connecting Indian startups with Airbus business units globally. The initiative focuses on partnering with innovative startups in aerospace, defence, AI, unmanned systems, industrial automation, sustainable aviation, connectivity, autonomy, and space technologies.

Selected startups receive unique opportunities, including collaboration with Airbus domain experts, strategic advisory, market access across Airbus business units, and a Proof of Concept fund for product validation. The program enables startups to develop scale-ready products with compelling business cases, supporting Airbus' 'local to global' strategic approach.
