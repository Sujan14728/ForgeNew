Industrial Innovation Practicum

Talent Enrichment In Industry

Forge.Lighthouse’s Industrial Innovation Practicum helps industrial companies upgrade their present skilled workforce, from operators to executives, into future-ready innovative talent, with practical tools and mindset of Innovation & Technology Management. Advanced industrial skills development services offered under Forge’s IP-IW (Innovation Practicum for Industrial Workforce) refreshes and empowers the industrial workforce, unlocking their potential to solve real-world problems and propel the industry to global competitiveness. This initiative bridges the gap between academic theory and on-the-ground application through the robust Practicum training model, ensuring industrial companies harness the full potential of the innovative workforce.

Labs

MAKING INDUSTRY INNOVATE, FASTER & BETTER

Forge.Lighthouse’s Industrial Innovation Labs enable industrial companies to innovate internally, by managing the implementation of Innovation Projects as a service, as well as enabling them to host their own Industrial Innovation Studios at significantly lower costs. These Labs democratise access to tools, resources and expertise, to translate innovative ideas into factory-scale industrial-grade solutions faster, better and cheaper via accelerated product development across the entire product innovation-compliance-certification-pilot production lifecycle.

Enterprise Innovation Services

Harness innovation capacity within Hardware Tech Startups, academia, and research by means of innovation sandboxes and labs, bootcamps and hackathons to source process innovations that enable them to achieve operational excellence and unlock business value; and product innovations that accelerate business growth, dominating existing markets and winning new markets.

TECHNOLOGY DEMO LABS

Showcase the application of emerging tech as operating capabilities for industrial use cases. By focusing on technologies with high potential but limited real-world adoption, TDLs aim to bridge the gap between innovation and implementation. While novelty is valued, TDLs prioritise the expansion of existing technologies into new domains.

TECHNOLOGY ADOPTION FRAMEWORK

How It Works?

1 Industrial innovation sandbox

Key challenges within industrial companies are identified and translated into detailed solution requirements, which are then hosted on the platform to attract solutions from startups.

2 TECH/SOLUTION DISCOVERY

Relevant startup solutions and technologies are sourced to address the specific needs of industrial companies. Startups submit applications on the platform, showcasing their capabilities to meet these challenges.

3 SCREENING & SELECTION

Startups and solutions undergo a screening and selection process, assisted by the platform, to identify high-potential candidates capable of delivering significant business value to industrial companies through pilot projects.

4 PILOT PROJECT EXECUTION & MONITORING

Real-time monitoring and tracking of pilot project execution by selected startups with industrial companies to demonstrate their solution’s ability to drive operational, business, and sustainability excellence.

5 Impact Studies

The platform tracks and measures the impact of these collaborations, evaluating key performance indicators (KPIs), impact metrics and other critical success criteria to assess the success of the projects.
