TECHNOLOGY ADOPTION FRAMEWORK

Forge.Lighthouse pioneers an Industrial Co-Innovation Platform that enables industrial enterprises to Discover, Validate and Partner/Procure innovative & technologically advanced innovations & solutions from startups via direct procurement, joint-product development and/or joint-industrialisation/GTM partnerships with these tech ventures.

How It Works?

1 Industrial innovation sandbox

Key challenges within industrial companies are identified and translated into detailed solution requirements, which are then hosted on the platform to attract solutions from startups.

2 TECH/SOLUTION DISCOVERY

Relevant startup solutions and technologies are sourced to address the specific needs of industrial companies. Startups submit applications on the platform, showcasing their capabilities to meet these challenges.

3 SCREENING & SELECTION

Startups and solutions undergo a screening and selection process, assisted by the platform, to identify high-potential candidates capable of delivering significant business value to industrial companies through pilot projects.

4 PIL OT PROJECT EXECUTION & MONITORING

Real-time monitoring and tracking of pilot project execution by selected startups with industrial companies to demonstrate their solution’s ability to drive operational, business, and sustainability excellence.

5 Impact Studies

The platform tracks and measures the impact of these collaborations, evaluating key performance indicators (KPIs), impact metrics and other critical success criteria to assess the success of the projects.
