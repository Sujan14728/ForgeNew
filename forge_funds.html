PRODUCT & STARTUP ACCELERATION

Despite hundreds of innovators coming up with innovative hardware solutions, these innovations often stall due to a valley of death between “Trial- ready prototypes”to “production ready MVPs”. Forge helps hardware product innovators & startup founders navigate this major challenge by offering access to technology labs, technical expertise, financial support, etc., to fast-track the innovators’ progress through product design & development, testing, certification, compliance stages and become ready for user-trials and paid pilots.

VENTURE BUILDER

Phy’gital (Hardware-First & Digital-Plus) startups have a huge potential in effectively addressing the needs and challenges of the public sectors, industrial companies, and MSMEs through innovative and technological products, but traditional procurement processes makes it challenging and financially unviable. Market Access Pathways are critical interventions that leverage open innovation & revamp procurement policies to bridge gaps between startups and public sector and Industrial companies - opening accelerated procurement pathways and enabling strategic partnerships.

The Venture Studio aspect of Forge.Fund’s interventions enables Startups to accelerate their outcomes through:

Startup Engagement Programs

Startup Partnership Models

Startup Engagement Programs that drive collaborative seeding & acceleration of innovative technologies & ventures by industrial majors via joint IP-generation/product delivery programs that enable startups and industrial companies alike to build enduring & sustainable competitive advantages in emerging technology domains - leveraging the other’s strengths to explore and capitalise on new market opportunities.

Startup Partnership Models that enable industrial majors to partner with and invest in creating high-growth tech startups and harvest from these assets through gains in profit (from operational excellence through tech sourced from startups) in revenue (generated from commercialising startup innovations) & in enterprise-value or market-capitalisation (from growth in equity valuation of startups invested in or acquired).

FUNDING PATHWAYS

Keeping in mind the pivotal role of capital in accelerating a startup’s growth, Forge.Fund provides catalytic capital via innovation grants and seed investments for PHY’GITAL tech (Hardware-First, Digital-Plus) startups - funding product development, product upgrades, product validation & certification, commercialisation & growth, to achieve critical milestones across Product-Factory Fit, Product-Market Fit and Product-Led Growth.

Industrial Sponsors

Industrial sponsors for Academic Research Projects [ARPs] to fund hardware product development via innovation & pilot grants for academic research projects with the potential to transform into hardware tech startups and Accelerated alignment with business value & industry use-cases.

Innovation Grants

Innovation Grants for product development, enabling promising prototypes to mature into market-ready solutions.

Pilot Grants

Pilot Grants to subsidise pilot deployments for the buyers/beneficiaries/users of startups’ tech reducing adoption risks while stimulating traction for industrial and impact innovations

GROWTH GRANTS & SEED INVESTMENTS

Growth Grants & Seed Investments to fuel product commercialisation, to scale GTM and vertically integrated operations.

INVESTMENT PATHWAYS & ADVISORY

Investment Pathways & Advisory to facilitate connections with Angel/VC investors with investment advisory across all aspects, from funding required to dilution in equity, financial planning, etc.
